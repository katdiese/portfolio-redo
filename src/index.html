Really nice to meet you.

I am a full stack developer in Denver, Colorado. Some of the technologies I use on a daily basis are Node, Express, Swig, Javascript, SQL & PostgreSQL, Knex.js, Sketch3, CSS3, Html5, JS, jQuery, Sass, Git & Github, Pivotal Tracker, and more. I not only love learning, but am pretty good at it (if I do say so myself). My preference is to work on a team, and I think that magic can happen when you pair program.

My favorite consideration when building an app is how what I'm building will impact the user. I am extremely passionate about usability and design, and think that everyone on a team touches the user experience in some way, so it should always be a priority.

What I love most about web development is how it entirely intersects creativity and logic.

If I'm not at my computer obsessing over some detail or another, I am probably getting lost in the mountains, learning to cook some new asian dish that I can't pronounce, or being manipulated by my cats into buying more kitty treats.

I am currently in the full stack dev program at Galvanize, which I will complete in June 2016.

  • Pet Store is an in progress app built as a group project.
